Privacy Policy – Balsam Mental Health App

Effective Date: September 10, 2025

1. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide:

  • Full name or alias.
  • Email address and phone number (if provided for registration or support).
  • Payment information (for paid subscriptions).
  • Inquiries, messages, or feedback you send via the app.
  • Any other data you choose to provide while using our services.

b) Usage Information:

  • Login data (timestamps, device type, OS, IP address).
  • In-app usage preferences (e.g., message counts, used sections).
  • Activity logs (conversations with the assistant, user interactions).

c) Technical Information and Cookies:

  • Cookies and similar technologies to personalize your experience and improve performance.
  • Anonymous analytics data for statistical and analytical purposes.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide services: enable chat, track your progress, and manage subscriptions.
  • Improve experience: personalize and enhance the app based on your needs.
  • Support: respond to inquiries via email or within the app.
  • Billing: process subscriptions and payments securely.
  • Legal: comply with applicable laws and prevent misuse.
  • Research & analytics: use anonymized data to improve digital mental health.

3. Sharing Information

  • We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share data with:
  • Service providers (payments, cloud storage, support).
  • Legal requirements (when compelled by law or to protect rights).
  • Mergers or acquisitions, ensuring continued privacy protection.

4. Data Security

  • We implement technical and organizational safeguards:
  • Encryption in transit and at rest.
  • Firewalls and regular monitoring.
  • Restricted employee access policies.
  • No system is 100% secure; absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

5. Data Retention

  • We retain data while your account is active or as needed to provide services.
  • Certain data may be retained longer for legal/accounting purposes.
  • You may request deletion at any time as outlined below.

6. Cookies

  • We use cookies to improve performance and user experience.
  • You can control cookies via browser settings; disabling may affect functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

  • Services are intended for adults (18+).
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children; if discovered, we delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

  • Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Rectify or update your data.
  • Request permanent deletion.
  • Object to certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent (without affecting prior lawful use).
  • Contact us to exercise these rights.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify you of material changes via the app or email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Medical Disclaimer

  • Balsam is a digital tool for general mental well-being and self-reflection.
  • It is not a substitute for professional medical or psychological advice.
  • We are not liable for decisions made based on app content or assistant conversations.
  • If you experience serious health issues, seek qualified professional help immediately.
  • In emergencies or thoughts of self-harm, contact local emergency services immediately.
